# How to Book a Parking Space

Reservations at SkyPark Central can be made through the chatbot, the website,
or by phone. Reservations are recommended during weekdays and major events.

## Information required

To create a reservation, we collect the following information from the guest:

1. **First name**
2. **Last name**
3. **Vehicle license plate number** (used to validate entry at the boom gate)
4. **Reservation period** — a start date/time and an end date/time

No payment information is collected by the chatbot. Payment is handled at the
exit kiosk or via the SkyPark mobile app.

## Confirmation flow

1. The chatbot collects the four fields above.
2. A **pending reservation** is created in the system.
3. A human administrator reviews and confirms the reservation (human-in-the-loop).
4. Upon confirmation, the guest receives a reservation code.

## Cancellation policy

- **Free cancellation** up to 2 hours before the reservation start time.
- **50% refund** for cancellations between 2 hours before and the start time.
- **No refund** after the reservation start time has passed.

## Modifying a reservation

Guests can modify the end time of an active reservation at any time through the
chatbot or the exit kiosk, subject to availability. Start times of pending
reservations can be modified up to 30 minutes before the original start.
