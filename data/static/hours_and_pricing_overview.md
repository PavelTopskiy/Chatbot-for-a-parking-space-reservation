# Hours and Pricing Overview

> Note: Live working hours, live space availability, and current pricing are
> served from the operational database. This document describes the general
> structure only; for exact numbers the chatbot queries the live system.

## Working hours (general)

SkyPark Central is open to vehicles **24 hours a day, 7 days a week**. Staffed
service at the main kiosk is available from **06:00 to 22:00** every day.
Outside of staffed hours, entry and exit are fully automated via the boom
gates and the mobile app.

## Pricing structure

Pricing depends on three factors:

1. **Vehicle type** — standard car, motorcycle, or oversized (SUV/van over 2.10 m).
2. **Duration** — hourly, daily, weekly, or monthly.
3. **Zone** — rooftop spaces are discounted compared to covered levels.

Hourly parking is billed in 15-minute increments after the first hour. Daily
rates apply from 00:00 to 23:59 of the same calendar day. Weekly and monthly
passes are available for commuters and residents; contact the office for
long-term contracts.

## Availability

Real-time space availability is published per zone (L1, L2, L3, Rooftop) and
refreshed every minute. EV charging bay availability is tracked separately
from standard L2 spaces.
