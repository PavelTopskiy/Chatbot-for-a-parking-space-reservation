# Rules and Policies

## General rules

- Maximum speed inside the garage is **10 km/h**.
- Drivers must obey the directional arrows and parking markings at all times.
- Each vehicle must occupy exactly one space. Double-parking is not allowed
  and will result in the vehicle being ticketed.
- Children must be accompanied by an adult at all times inside the garage.
- Smoking and open flames are strictly prohibited on all levels.

## Prohibited items and vehicles

- Vehicles taller than 2.10 m are only allowed on the rooftop level.
- Commercial vehicles over 3.5 tonnes are not accepted.
- Storage of flammable materials, propane tanks, or hazardous goods in parked
  vehicles is prohibited.
- Overnight storage of trailers without prior arrangement is not allowed.

## Lost ticket

If a guest loses their entry ticket, a **flat lost-ticket fee of $35** applies in
addition to the maximum daily rate. The guest must present a valid ID and the
vehicle registration at the exit kiosk.

## Damage and liability

SkyPark Central is not liable for theft of, or damage to, vehicles or their
contents. Guests are advised to lock their vehicles and not leave valuables
visible. Incidents should be reported immediately to on-site security and,
if needed, to the local police.

## Accessibility

Accessible spaces are reserved for vehicles displaying a valid disability permit.
Misuse of accessible spaces is subject to towing and a $250 fine.
