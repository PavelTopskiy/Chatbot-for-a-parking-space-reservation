# About SkyPark Central

SkyPark Central is a modern multi-level parking facility located in the heart of
downtown Rivertown. The garage has **450 total parking spaces** distributed across
three covered levels and one rooftop level.

## Key facts

- **Operator:** SkyPark Holdings Inc.
- **Opened:** 2019
- **Total capacity:** 450 vehicles
- **Levels:** L1 (standard), L2 (EV charging + standard), L3 (standard + oversized),
  Rooftop (uncovered, discounted).
- **Maximum vehicle height:** 2.10 meters on L1–L3. The rooftop has no height limit.
- **EV charging:** 24 Level-2 chargers on L2 (Type 2 / CCS). Charging is billed
  separately from parking.
- **Accessible spaces:** 18 ADA-compliant spaces located near each elevator bank.
- **Security:** 24/7 CCTV, on-site security staff, and well-lit stairwells.
- **Amenities:** Two elevators, restrooms on L1, a small convenience kiosk near the
  main entrance, and a free shuttle to Rivertown Central Station every 15 minutes.
