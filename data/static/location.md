# Location and Directions

## Address

SkyPark Central
120 Harbor Avenue
Rivertown, RT 10421

## Neighborhood

SkyPark Central sits on the corner of Harbor Avenue and 4th Street, two blocks
south of Rivertown Central Station and directly across from the Harborfront Mall.
Popular nearby destinations include the Rivertown Convention Center (5 min walk),
the Old Pier district (10 min walk), and the Financial Quarter (15 min walk).

## By car

- **From the north (Highway 9):** Take exit 14B toward Harbor Avenue, continue
  south for 1.2 km, SkyPark Central will be on your right after 4th Street.
- **From the south (Highway 12):** Take exit 3 toward Downtown, follow Market
  Street for 800 m, turn right onto Harbor Avenue.
- **Entrance:** The vehicle entrance is on 4th Street. Do not attempt to enter
  from Harbor Avenue — that side is pedestrian-only.

## By public transit

- **Train:** Rivertown Central Station is a 3-minute walk. Lines R1, R2, and
  the airport express all stop there.
- **Bus:** Routes 14, 22, and 88 stop directly in front of the Harborfront Mall.

## Shuttle

SkyPark Central operates a free shuttle between the garage and Rivertown Central
Station every 15 minutes from 06:00 to 23:00 daily.
