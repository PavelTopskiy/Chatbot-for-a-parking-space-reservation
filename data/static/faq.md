# Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I enter the garage at 3 AM?**
A: Yes. SkyPark Central is open 24/7. Outside staffed hours (06:00–22:00)
entry and exit are handled automatically by the boom gates.

**Q: Do you have EV charging?**
A: Yes, 24 Level-2 chargers (Type 2 / CCS) are available on Level 2. Charging
is billed separately from parking.

**Q: My car is 2.3 m tall. Where can I park?**
A: Vehicles taller than 2.10 m must use the rooftop level, which has no
height limit.

**Q: Do I need a reservation?**
A: Reservations are recommended during weekdays, concerts at the Convention
Center, and holidays. Walk-in parking is always available subject to
availability.

**Q: How do I pay?**
A: At the exit kiosk by credit card or contactless, or through the SkyPark
mobile app. The chatbot does not collect payment information.

**Q: Is the garage safe?**
A: Yes. The garage has 24/7 CCTV, on-site security staff, and well-lit
stairwells and elevators.

**Q: Can I leave my car overnight?**
A: Yes, overnight parking is allowed. Daily and weekly rates apply.

**Q: What if I lose my ticket?**
A: A $35 lost-ticket fee applies in addition to the maximum daily rate.
Present a valid ID and vehicle registration at the exit kiosk.

**Q: Do you have accessible parking?**
A: Yes, 18 ADA-compliant spaces are located near each elevator bank.

**Q: How far is the train station?**
A: Rivertown Central Station is a 3-minute walk, and a free shuttle runs
every 15 minutes between 06:00 and 23:00.
